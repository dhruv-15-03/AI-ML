ML- Machine Learning , basically we enable the machine to learn from its outcome.
3 types of ML
1.) Supervised- Supervised and output is corrected by humans (given the correct answer).
2.) Unsupervised - Not supervised learn from its own output (basically grouping or clustering).
3.) Reinforcement - Reward/punishment
